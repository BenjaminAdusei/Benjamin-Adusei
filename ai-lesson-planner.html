🤖 AI Lesson Planner

Serverless AI tool that generates WAEC-aligned lesson plans for Ghanaian teachers

🚀 Launch Live Demo

The Problem

Lesson planning takes 40–60 minutes per subject. Many teachers lack structured templates aligned with WAEC standards.

My Solution

A serverless web app using Groq's Llama 3.3 70B model. Enter topic, class, duration, and available resources – get a complete lesson plan with learning objectives, step‑by‑step activities, assessment, and local examples (e.g., cocoa farming, market scenes).

Tech Stack

HTML/CSS/JS Netlify Functions Groq API (Llama 3.3) Environment Variables

Impact

Reduces lesson planning time from ~45 minutes to under 2 minutes. Piloted with 6 JHS teachers who confirmed plans are "ready to use" with minor tweaks.
